Business Problem:
Food delivery platforms often provide inaccurate delivery time estimates, leading to customer dissatisfaction and
operational inefficiencies. This project aims to predict food delivery time and identify key factors that cause delivery delays. 

Beneficiaries:
The model benefits food delivery platform by improving delivery planning and enhances customer experience through more reliable time estimates.

Decision Supported:
The model supports decisions such as identifying high-delay-risk orders and setting realistic delivery expectations for customers.

Goal:
The primary goal is to reduce delivery delays, with accurate time prediction.
